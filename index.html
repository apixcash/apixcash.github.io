Frequently Asked Question

What is APIXCASH?

The APIXCASH Project started 22nd Sep 2022 02:28:30 when the 1st genesis block got mined.
It is a fork of the Ravencoin code & FoxdCoin
We will follow their development and add experimental alternative features. This project is opensource and everyone is welcome to contribute.

How do you secure your wallet?

Good safety measures include encrypting your desktop wallet with a password and for extra security keep a backup of at least the wallet.dat in a secure location (preferably an external drive) If you wish to keep your foxd coins offline, download a paper wallet from https://paper.apix.cash and send your coins from your desktop wallet to the paper wallet and keep this paper wallet safe
