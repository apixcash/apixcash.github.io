APIX CASH Project

APIX CASH (APIX) project launched on 22th, Sept 2022, when the genesis block got mined, We are an open-source project designed to enable instant payments to ayone around the world.

The aim of the project is to create a blockhain optimized specifically for the transfer of assets such as coins from one holder to another

The project has No pre-mine, No ICO, or Masternode

The APIXCASH Project started 22nd Sep 2022 02:28:30 when the 1st genesis block got mined.

It is a fork of the Ravencoin code & FoxdCoin We will follow their development and add experimental alternative features. This project is opensource and everyone is welcome to contribute.

Good safety measures include encrypting your desktop wallet with a password and for extra security keep a backup of at least the wallet.dat in a secure location (preferably an external drive) If you wish to keep your foxd coins offline, download a paper wallet from https://paper.apix.cash and send your coins from your desktop wallet to the paper wallet and keep this paper wallet safe
